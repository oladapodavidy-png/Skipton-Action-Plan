The standing order runs itself. A 6 month reminder keeps you in control and lets you increase as your income grows.

✓
Set a phone reminder for 6 months from today to review your balance

The long game: £100 per month saves around £18,000 in interest. Increasing to £200 saves roughly £32,000. Every consistent £100 extra makes a significant difference over 30 years.
